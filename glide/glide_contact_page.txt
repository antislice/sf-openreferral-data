<h3>Map and Directions</h3>
<div>
<div>
<p>Directions are available through Google Maps, click <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?client=firefox-a&amp;rls=org.mozilla:en-US:official&amp;channel=s&amp;hl=en&amp;q=330+ellis+street+san+francisco&amp;um=1&amp;ie=UTF-8&amp;hq=&amp;hnear=330+Ellis+St,+San+Francisco,+CA+94102&amp;gl=us&amp;ei=6mfTTMNEkOaxA9aNwbgO&amp;sa=X&amp;oi=geocode_result&amp;ct=title&amp;resnum=1&amp;ved=0CBMQ8gEwAA">here</a>.</p>

<div><strong><br /></strong></div>
<div>
<p><strong>From the South Bay</strong></p>
<p>Exit Highway 101 at 7th Street. Go straight on Bryant, turning left on 6th Street. At Market Street, 6th Street becomes Taylor Street. Follow Taylor three blocks further; Glide is on your left.</p>
<p>&#160;</p>
<p><strong>From the Marin County</strong></p>
<p>Cross the Golden Gate Bridge, exiting on Lombard Street. Turn right on Van Ness Avenue, left on O'Farrell Street, right on Mason, and right again on Ellis Street. Glide is one block up.</p>
<p><strong>&#160;</strong></p>
<p><strong>From the East Bay</strong></p>
<p>Take the Bay Bridge, exiting on 5th Street. At Market Street, 5th Street becomes Cyril Magnin. Turn left on Ellis; Glide is two blocks further.</p>
<p>&#160;</p>
<h3>Public Transportation Information</h3>
<p>If you are riding BART or MUNI, exit at the Powell Street Station  (located at Market and Powell Streets). Walk north one block on Powell  Street, turning left on Ellis Street. Glide is three blocks further on  the right. The #27 MUNI bus stops in front of Glide on Ellis Street.</p>
<p>&#160;</p>
<h3><a name="parking"> </a>Parking near GLIDE</h3>
<p>APM Parking Garage is located at 261 Ellis Street between Mason and Taylor. Windsor Parking/ExecuPark is located at the corner of Taylor and Eddy (sign reads Discount Parking).</p>
